<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for someone who is unconscious and not breathing</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: The delivery of chest compressions</font></h4>
<p>1. Check breathing by tilting their head backwards and looking and feeling for breaths.<p>
<p>2. Call 1199 as soon as possible, or get someone else to do it.<p>
<p>3. Push firmly downwards in the middle of the chest and then release.<p>
<p>4. Push at a regular rate until help arrives.<p>


<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for someone who’s unconscious and breathing</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Place the person on their side and tilt their head back</font></h4>
<p><font color="#F44336">1.</font><font color="#3F51B5"> Check breathing by tilting their head backwards and looking and feeling for breaths.</font><p>
