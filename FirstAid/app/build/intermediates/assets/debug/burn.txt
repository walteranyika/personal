<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for burns</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Cool the affected area</font></h4>
<p>1. Cool the burn under cold running water for at least ten minutes.<p>
<p>2. Remove all clothing and jewellery from the
affected area, unless it is attached to the burn<p>
<p>3. Loosely cover the burn with cling film or a clean plastic bag.<p>
<p>4. Avoid using any creams or lotions.<p>
<p>5. If necessary,or get someone else to do it.<p>
