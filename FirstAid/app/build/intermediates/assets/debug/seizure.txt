<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for seizures (epilepsy)</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Make them safe and prevent injury</font></h4>
<p><font color="#F44336">1.</font> <font color="#3F51B5">Do not restrain them but use a blanket or clothing to protect their head from injury.</font><p>
<p><font color="#F44336">2.</font> <font color="#3F51B5">After the seizure, help the person rest on their side with their head tilted back.</font><p>
