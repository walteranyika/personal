<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for a stroke</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Carry out the FAST test</font></h4>
<p>1. Think FAST. 
<font color="#F44336">Face: </font><font color="#4CAF50">is there weakness on one side of the face?</font> 
<font color="#F44336">Arms: </font><font color="#4CAF50">can they raise both arms?</font>
<font color="#F44336">Speech:</font> <font color="#4CAF50">is their speech easily understood?</font>
<p>Time: to call 1199.</font><p>
<p>2. <font color="#4CAF50">Immediately or get someone else to do it.</font><p>
