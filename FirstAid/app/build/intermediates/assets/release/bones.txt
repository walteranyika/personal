<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for a broken bone</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Immobilise the affected part</font></h4>
<p>1. Encourage the person to support the injury with their hand, or use a cushion or items
of clothing to prevent unnecessary movement.<p>
<p>2. As soon as possible,or get someone else to do it.<p>
<p>3. Continue supporting the injury until help arrives.<p>
