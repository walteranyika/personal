<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for a head injury</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Apply something cold</font></h4>
<p>1. Ask them to rest and apply a cold compress to the injury (e.g. frozen vegetables wrapped
in a tea towel).<p>
<p>2. If they become drowsy or vomit,or get someone else to do it.<p>
