<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for poisoning and harmful substance</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Establish what? When? And how much?</font></h4>
<p>1. <font color="#3F51B5">Establish what they have taken. When? And how much?</font><p>
<p>2. <font color="#3F51B5">As soon as possible, or get someone else to do it.</font><p>
<p>3. <font color="#3F51B5">Do not make the person sick.</font><p>
