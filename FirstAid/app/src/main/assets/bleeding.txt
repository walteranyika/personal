<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First aid for someone who is bleeding heavily</font></h2>
<h4><font color="#F44336">Key skill: Put pressure on the wound</font></h4>
<p>1. Put pressure on the wound with whatever is available to stop or slow down the flow of blood.<p>
<p>2. As soon as possible,or get someone else to do it.<p>
<p>3. Keep pressure on the wound until help arrives.<p>
