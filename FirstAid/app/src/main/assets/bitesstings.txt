<h2><font color="#4CAF50">First Aid for Bites and Stings</font></h2>

<h4><font color="#F44336">General Signs and Symptoms associated with bites and stings:</font></h4>
<p>1. Redness<p>
<p>2. Swelling<p>
<p>3. Pain</br><p>
<p>4. Itching<p>
<p>5. Nausea<p>
<p>6. Problems breathing<p>

<h4><font color="#F44336">First Aid</font></h4>
<p>1.Remove jewelry and constrictive clothing<p>
<p>2.Wash the area with soap and clean water<p>
<p>3.Cover the area with an adhesive bandage or gauze wrap<p>
<p>4.Apply ice if needed to reduce pain and swelling<p>
